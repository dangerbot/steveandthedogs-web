Why this is different.
Account in one step
Phone or email. No password. No re-login. Today's flow asks for both, plus a password, plus makes you sign in again after verification.
Three real paths, not nine taps
Set up now, waiting for it, or just exploring. Today's flow funnels everyone through pick-kit, confirm-kit, pick-model, confirm-model — even if your Gardyn isn't here yet.
Kelby leads the setup
Meet Kelby, name your garden, pick starter plants. Today's flow hides Kelby behind the paywall — users pay for an AI they've never met.
One membership, three cadences
No feature comparison grid. No "Are you sure?" trap modal. Free tier stays useful: one Kelby check-in a day, always.
Free actually means free
"Continue with free for now" actually works. No grayed-out tabs. No paywall pushed back at you tomorrow.
How I built this.

I walked the current Gardyn onboarding end-to-end on my phone — 22 screens from welcome to home — and logged every point of friction. Then I worked iteratively with Claude to sketch a cleaner version: structure first, then voice, then visuals tuned to Gardyn's brand. Single HTML file, hosted as an unlisted page off my site.
